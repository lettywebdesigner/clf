About the project

The mission of the Christine Lompo Foundation is to improve the quality of life in Burkina Faso (West Africa) by providing educational and social services to people in need as well as providing opportunities for career building and training.

The Christine Lompo Foundation plans to expand by providing career services to the needy families in order to help them acquire new life skills and ultimately gain employment.

BURKINA FASO

Burkina Faso also called "the country of honest people" is a beautiful landlocked country in West Africa. Despite the government efforts, the literacy rate is among the lowest in the world with only 30 percent. This means that only 30 percent of the entire population above age 15 can read and write. The female literacy rate is even worst. the low literacy rate explains the high teenage pregnancy rates and the extremely high level of poverty in Burkina Faso.
